Nyclyter

A Game of Mystery and Intrigue

About

In "Nyclyter," you and a partner step into the roles of detectives exploring an abandoned town filled with dark secrets and unsolved mysteries. Together, you must uncover clues, analyze strange happenings, and rely on each other's strengths to solve the puzzles that await.

As a team, you will search forgotten locations, engage with enigmatic characters, and piece together fragments of a hidden story. Every decision brings you closer to the truth or leads you down paths with unexpected consequences. Prepare yourselves for complex mental challenges, immersive atmospheres, and an experience that requires trust and teamwork.
