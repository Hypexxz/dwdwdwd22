Nyclyter

A Game of Mystery and Intrigue

About

In "Nyclyter," you are a detective venturing into an abandoned town shrouded in dark secrets and unsolved mysteries. Each clue you find, each area you investigate, draws you deeper into a web of intrigue, where nothing is quite as it seems.

Explore forgotten locations, interact with mysterious characters, and uncover fragments of a hidden story as you attempt to solve the town's disappearances. Prepare yourself for mental challenges, complex puzzles, and an atmosphere that will keep you on edge with every step.
